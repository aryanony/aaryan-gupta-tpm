Q: Why do EMR implementations usually fail in clinics?

A: Most EMR software forces the doctor to click 15 times just to write a simple paracetamol prescription. This slows down consultation times. I map EMR systems to clinical speed, optimizing drug templating and leveraging barcode scanners or voice-to-text input to keep OPD times under 3 minutes per patient.
